The Perfect Blend of Beauty and Functionality

Earthos is our premium interior microcement formulation, specifically engineered to create elegant, seamless surfaces with organic textures that bring warmth and sophistication to any space.

Unlike traditional tiling or concrete flooring, Earthos provides a continuous surface without joints or seams, creating a modern, clean aesthetic while being highly resistant to water, stains, and wear. Its unique formulation allows for application on floors, walls, countertops, showers, and more.

Each Earthos installation is a bespoke work of art, with subtle variations in texture and finish that give spaces a distinctive, handcrafted character while maintaining consistent quality and durability.

Earthos Maintenance Guidelines

Keep your Earthos surfaces looking beautiful for years with these simple care tips:

Daily Cleaning

Clean with a pH-neutral cleaner and soft microfiber cloth. Avoid abrasive cleaners or scrubbing pads that could damage the surface.

Spill Response

While Earthos is stain-resistant, wipe up spills promptly, especially acidic substances like wine or lemon juice to maintain the surface finish.

Furniture Protection

Use furniture pads to prevent scratches on floor applications. Though durable, Earthos benefits from reasonable care to preserve its beauty.

Periodic Maintenance

We recommend a professional maintenance treatment every 3-5 years to refresh the sealant and preserve the microcement's protective properties.

High Traffic Areas

Consider using area rugs in extremely high-traffic zones to extend the life of your Earthos flooring while maintaining its aesthetic appeal.

Minor Repairs

In the rare event of damage, contact our team for professional repairs. Earthos can be spot-repaired without needing to redo the entire surface.
